Red Card, Blue Card

Let's play a game! You'll get $10 each time you win. If I win, you'll lose $5 and one happiness point.

I'll play first. To stay in the game, you'll need to play a red card that's higher than mine, or a blue card that's lower than mine. If none of your cards feel like a good choice, you can take your chances and play a random card from the deck.

After your turn, I'll play again. I will have to play a red card that's higher than your last played card, or a blue card that's lower. If I don't, then I lose.
